UE Manila - Society of Computer Engineering Students

A Computer Engineering organization in University of the East - Manila. It explores different topics about technologies, hardware, software, programming, etc. Also, it helps the students to be more comfortable about the Computer Engineering program.

General Assembly

Computer Engineering students in different years are gathering. In a general assembly, many events are happening like talks, special numbers, games, etc. The most important thing in a G.A is making friends with other year levels.

Schedules and Announcements

The organization helps the students to know the schedules and announcements that the school gives. Also, they update the students about the things that will happen inside and outside the organization.

Workshops and Trainings

Free workshops and trainings are also offered in the organization. Different types of workshops are given, for example Arduino workshop, Tech Crush, Leadership training, etc.
